# Swatch — Research Brief

## Market Overview

The color management software market is ~$1.4B (2024), projected to hit $2.5-3B by 2033 (9%+ CAGR). Growth driven by cloud-based color tools, digital printing expansion, and brand consistency demands.

**Key event:** In late 2022, Adobe removed Pantone libraries from Creative Cloud. Designers who had free Pantone access now need Pantone Connect ($90/year). This created a massive, angry, underserved market.

## Competitor Analysis

| Tool | What It Does | Pricing | Strengths | Weaknesses |
|------|-------------|---------|-----------|------------|
| Pantone Connect | Official Pantone platform + Adobe plugin | $90/yr | Official data, 15K+ colors | Universally hated — buggy, inaccurate, overpriced, terrible UX |
| Coolors | Fast palette generator from images | Free / €3/mo pro | Beautiful UX, beloved by designers | No Pantone matching at all |
| Adobe Color | Image → color themes, harmony tools | Free w/ Adobe CC | Tight Adobe integration | Pantone matching requires separate Pantone Connect sub |
| ColorSnap | Photo-to-paint matching (Sherwin-Williams only) | Free | Good AR, polished mobile | Single brand only, not for design professionals |
| Free tools (PrintKick, GiniFab) | Click a pixel, get nearest Pantone | Free | Zero cost | One color at a time, crude UX, no palette extraction |

## Gap Analysis

1. **No tool does image → paint palette across multiple brands.** ColorSnap is Sherwin-Williams only.
2. **No tool does image → Pantone palette in one step.** Today it's a 2-3 tool workflow.
3. **Pantone Connect is hated but has no real competitor.**
4. **Interior designers are especially underserved** — need paint-brand-specific codes, not just hex/RGB.

## Target User Profile — Interior Design / Paint Focus

| Segment | Need | Where They Are |
|---------|------|----------------|
| Interior designers | Match inspiration photos to paint across brands | r/InteriorDesign, Houzz, Pinterest, Instagram |
| Home renovators | "What paint is this color?" from photos | r/homeimprovement, Pinterest, TikTok |
| Paint contractors | Verify client color requests against available paints | Signs101, PrintPlanet, industry forums |

## Go/No-Go

**Strong go.** Real demand, weak competition, angry users looking for alternatives. Multi-brand paint matching is a clear differentiator no one else offers.

**Key risk:** Pantone protects its color data aggressively. V0.1 uses approximation-based matching with a disclaimer, not official licensing.
